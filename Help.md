# Partly Commands

## Math
**+add (a) (b)** adds two numbers, a and b

**+multiply (a) (b)** multipies two numbers, a and b

**+divide (a) (b)** divides two numbers, a and b

**+subtract (a) (b)** subtracts two numbers, a and b
